# Installer Terms

The public OSS launch should keep installer terms simple and accurate:

- The installer is distributed under the Apache-2.0 license.
- The repo must not embed private support contacts or private infrastructure references.
- Users should only be asked for the minimum information needed to install, run, or debug the app.
- Any future distribution terms must be published separately and must not override the OSS license or README.
