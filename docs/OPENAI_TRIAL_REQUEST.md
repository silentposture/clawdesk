# ChatGPT Pro Trial Request Pack

## Official status

- ChatGPT Pro is a paid subscription plan at $200/month.
- OpenAI’s public Help Center says free trials and promotional subscriptions are invite- and eligibility-based.
- Eligible users may see invite options in email or within ChatGPT; details vary by campaign.
- Some campaigns require a payment method to start the trial and may auto-renew unless canceled before the end date.

Official references:
- [ChatGPT Pricing](https://openai.com/chatgpt/pricing)
- [ChatGPT Pro introduction](https://openai.com/index/introducing-chatgpt-pro/)
- [Free Trial Invites FAQ](https://help.openai.com/en/articles/8381046-free-trial-invites-faq)

## What that means

- There is no public self-serve application page documented in the official sources for a ChatGPT Pro promotional free trial.
- The practical path is:
  1. Keep the account in good standing.
  2. Watch email and the ChatGPT product for invite options.
  3. If an invite or promo appears, redeem it from the product flow.
  4. If you want to ask for consideration, send a concise support/product-feedback message.

## Checklist before sending

- Confirm the account is eligible for the specific campaign.
- If the promo requires a payment method, be ready to review it before redemption.
- Keep the message concise and do not claim entitlement.
- Use the wording above only if the current OpenAI flow offers a support or feedback path.

## Notes

- The free-trial program is invitation- and eligibility-based, so this request does not guarantee approval.
- If you already have a paid ChatGPT plan, you may not be eligible for many invite campaigns.
- If you want the $200 Pro plan directly, use the Pricing page or Settings → My Plan instead of waiting for a promo invite.
