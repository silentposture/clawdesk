# Code of Conduct

ClawDesk contributors are expected to be respectful, precise, and honest.

## Expected behavior

- Be constructive and specific in feedback.
- Keep discussion focused on the code, docs, and release posture.
- Respect redaction requirements and security boundaries.
- Assume good intent, but do not ignore unsafe or misleading claims.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, intimidation, or exclusionary language.
- Sharing secrets, personal data, private endpoints, or unreleased customer information.
- Pushing unreviewed automation that bypasses human approval.
- Misrepresenting the project status, adoption, security, or license.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may close issues, reject PRs, or remove collaboration privileges when behavior breaks the rules above.
If you need to report a conduct issue, open a private discussion or use the maintainers' conduct contact if one is published for the release.
